### üó∫Ô∏è Environment & Levels
The game features a tutorial and 6 progressively difficult levels:
1. **Trial (Elf Bootcamp):** A safe "guide book" environment to learn movement and shooting mechanics.
2. **Level 1 (Snowy Courtyard):** Beginner level fighting slow-moving Ice Slimes.
3. **Level 2 (The Mail Room):** Fast-paced action where hitting flying letters provides speed boosts.
4. **Level 3 (Toy Assembly Line):** Event-driven gameplay involving moving conveyor belts.
5. **Level 4 (The Wrapping Station):** Obstacle-heavy arena requiring players to dodge sticky tape traps.
6. **Level 5 (Engine Room):** Expert level featuring steam vents and stamina-drain mechanics.
7. **Level 6 (The Main Vault):** Final Boss fight against a giant Robotic Nutcracker.
